Lemon Pastry Cream
Ingredients:
1	egg
2	egg yolks
½ cup	white sugar
¼ cup	cornstarch
2 cups	whole milk
1	vanilla bean
50g	tempered white chocolate
300ml	heavy cream
Procedure:
Add egg, yolk, sugar, cornstarch and salt into a bowl; Whisk until thickened
Add vanilla bean into a pot with milk; bring to simmer
Temper the egg mixture into the pot with milk
Continue to whisk over low-medium heat until thickened and corn starch is cooked
Pass through a fine sieve
Fold in whipped cream and tempered chocolate
Wrap to prevent a skin from forming; chill and store
